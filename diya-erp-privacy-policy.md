# Diya-Erp-Privacy-Policy

Effective Date: February 2025
Last Updated: February 2025

Diya ERP is committed to protecting the privacy of students, teachers and educational authorities. This Privacy Policy explains how the Diya ERP application collects, uses, and safeguards data for school administration and academic operations.

1. Data We Collect
Basic profile details (name, class/section, school, and institutional role).
Institution-issued login ID and account access information.
Academic and ERP records such as attendance, marks, timetable, fees, notices, and remarks.
Operational records such as grievance submissions, activity logs, and usage diagnostics.
2. Why We Collect Data
We use this information to:

Deliver ERP services for schools, teachers, students, and administrators.
Manage attendance, academics, communication, and school workflows.
Generate reports, dashboards, and compliance records for authorized institutions.
Maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and improve service quality.
3. Data Sharing
We share data only with authorized schools, departments, and education authorities linked to your institution. We do not sell personal data and do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Children’s Privacy
Student data is processed only under school or guardian-authorized educational use. Diya ERP supports institutional supervision and follows applicable child data protection requirements under Indian law and other relevant regulations.

5. Data Security
Data is protected with encryption during transmission and secure cloud storage.
Role-based access controls and authentication are used to prevent unauthorized access.
Administrative and technical safeguards are applied to protect institutional records.
6. User Rights
Users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data through their school/institution administrator or by contacting us directly.

7. Contact
For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact: support@diyaind.com
